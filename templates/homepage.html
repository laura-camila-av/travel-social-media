About Tripster

Built by students, for students

Tripster is a community built for students who want practical and honest travel ideas. Planning a trip during semester can take too much time, so this platform helps travellers share real experiences and help others skip the research spiral.

Whether you're a seasoned backpacker or planning your first solo trip, you can explore detailed itineraries from people just like you.

Join the community, share your adventures, and help another student find their next destination.
