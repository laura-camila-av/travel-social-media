About Trampled

This is a community built by students, for students. We know that planning a trip takes time you don't have during semester, so we built a place where travellers can share their real experiences and help the next person skip the research spiral.

Whether you're a seasoned backpacker or planning your first solo trip, we give you access to honest, detailed itineraries from people just like you. No sponsored content, no travel agencies — just real students sharing what actually worked.

Join the community, share your adventures, and help a fellow student find their next destination.
