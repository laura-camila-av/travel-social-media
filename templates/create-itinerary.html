How to use this form

  1. Enter your trip title, name, destination, travel style, start date, end date, and budget.
  2. Click Generate Itinerary Days.
  3. Open each day tile to fill in activities, dining, transport, accommodation, cost, rented items, and photo.
  4. Click Save Progress any time to continue later.
  5. When finished, submit the itinerary.
